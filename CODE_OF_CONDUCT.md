# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Helping others debug hardware issues
- Sharing test results from different Apollo models
- Accepting feedback gracefully

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Posting pirated UA software or circumvention tools
- Spamming issues or discussions

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, wiki, and any communication channels associated with the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to **rolotrealanis@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and addressed.
